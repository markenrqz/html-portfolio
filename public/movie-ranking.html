The Best K-Dramas According to Mark

My top 3 K-Dramas of all-time.

Reply 1988

Five childhood friends, who all live in the same Ssangmundong neighborhood of Seoul in 1988, lean on each other to survive their challenging teen years and set a path for their futures.

Twinkling Watermelon

Eun-gyeol is a model student by day, but at night he leads a double life as a guitarist in a band. After having an argument with his father, where his father learned of his musical interest, he time slips to 1995 and meets his parents when they were younger.

Queen of Tears

The drama stars attorney Baek Hyunwoo (Kim Soohyun), and his wife Hong Haein (Kim Jiwon), heiress chaebol CEO of the Queens Group department store. The story follows their journey as they navigate their marital crisis
